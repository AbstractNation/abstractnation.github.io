Biography

Danil Skvortsov "was born on July 25, 2005" better known as "Abstract Nation" (commonly incorrectly referred to as "Habstrakt Nation"), a Russian music producer based in Ulyanovsk, Russia.

As an independent producer, Abstract Nation is influenced by Geoxor, Exile, VRIME, Habstrakt, The Dual Personality, HYPRESSION, Hybrid Screech and many more.
